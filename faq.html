Is Twitch Prime required to receive items?

No, Twitch Prime is not required to receive items during this event. Simply link your Twitch and Steam accounts and watch participating Dota 2 streams to be eligible for items.

We're giving out prizes to create an engaging and rewarding experience for viewers, promote the Dota 2 category on Twitch, and support our partners. By offering exclusive items and other prizes, we aim to increase viewership, encourage interaction, and strengthen the Dota 2 community.
